Mombasa. Feels like yesterday, i'm standing infront of the entrance to Fort Jesus. I can still smell the cold breeze blow past my face. The aroma of being free. staring at so much water. I wanna go back lke yesterday.

The year was 2019 and i was on a work trip turned adventure trip. I can say it was the year of doing quite a lot of things for the very first time, First time i'm stepping outside my country Uganda, first time am on a plane, first time i'm living in an apartment, first time a monkey is stealing my breakfast, i do miss that monkey though, hihihi, first time i'm sleeping with the AC on coz its too hot, first time for a lot of things.

I made a bucket list, who want to be my travel buddy coz every year i gotta go see the world.
